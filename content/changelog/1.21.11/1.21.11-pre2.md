# Changes

  * When the "See-Through Leaves" video setting is disabled, the gaps between leaves are now dark green instead of black
  * Turning the "See-Through Leaves" video setting off now gives a bigger performance boost than before

# Fixed bugs in 1.21.11 Pre-Release 2

  * [MC-238715](https://mojira.dev/MC-238715) The "minecraft:block.chest.locked" sound cannot be heard by other players when attempting to open locked blocks
  * [MC-302660](https://mojira.dev/MC-302660) The "options.cutoutLeaves.tooltip" string is missing the word “you” making it grammatically incorrect
  * [MC-303061](https://mojira.dev/MC-303061) The "minecraft:block.chest.locked" sound plays at the player position, not the chest's
  * [MC-304023](https://mojira.dev/MC-304023) Redundant level data fixer files are saved in worlds' "data" folder
  * [MC-304369](https://mojira.dev/MC-304369) Crash when loading the game without an existing options.txt file
  * [MC-304401](https://mojira.dev/MC-304401) Non see-through leaves look different compared to the previous snapshot
  * [MC-304421](https://mojira.dev/MC-304421) "attack_range" component breaks hit result
